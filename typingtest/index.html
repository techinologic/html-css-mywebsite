Odessa Rose received her B.A. in English from Coppin State University and her M.A. in Literature from the University of Maryland at College Park. She is a recipient of the Just About Books Annual Book and The African American Authors Expo Awards. Her first novel, Water In A Broken Glass, captured the #6 spot on the On-Demand Best Seller list, was recorded for the Maryland School for the Blind, is on Towson University’s Rainbow Lounge and Resource Library booklist, is included in The Greenwood Encyclopedia of Multiethnic American Literature, and is ranked #17 on Accredited Online Colleges’ 20 Essential Novels For African-American Women list.
